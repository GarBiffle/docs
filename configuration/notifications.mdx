Company Brain keeps your team informed through in-app notifications, email digests, and Slack alerts so important knowledge updates never go unnoticed. You control the signal-to-noise ratio — choose exactly which events trigger a notification and through which channel they reach you.

## Notification types

Company Brain delivers notifications through three channels, each suited to different urgency levels and working styles.

### 1. In-app notifications

Click the **bell icon** in the top-right corner of the app to open your notifications panel. In-app notifications are triggered by:

- **Mentions** — someone typed `@yourusername` in a knowledge item or comment.
- **Followed item updates** — an item or space you follow has been edited or has new comments.
- **Space announcements** — a Space Admin posted an announcement to a space you're a member of.

Unread notifications are indicated by a red badge on the bell icon. Click any notification to jump directly to the relevant item.

### 2. Email notifications

Email notifications deliver a digest of new and updated knowledge items from your spaces. You can choose to receive them **daily** (sent at 8 AM in your timezone) or **weekly** (sent every Monday morning). Email notifications are off by default and must be enabled in your personal notification settings.

### 3. Slack notifications

Send automated alerts to a Slack channel when items are added or updated in a specific space. Slack notifications are configured per-space (not per-user) and require the Slack integration to be connected. See [Set Up and Manage Integration Connections](/configuration/integrations-setup) for instructions on connecting Slack.

## Configuring your personal notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your avatar menu">
    Click your **avatar** (profile picture or initials) in the top-right corner of the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Notification Settings">
    Click **Notification Settings** in the dropdown menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle notification types">
    Turn each notification type on or off using the toggles:
    - In-app notifications (mentions, follows, announcements)
    - Email notifications
    - Browser push notifications (if enabled in your browser)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set email digest frequency">
    If email notifications are enabled, select your preferred digest frequency: **Daily**, **Weekly**, or **Off**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your preferences">
    Click **Save preferences**. Your settings take effect immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configuring Slack alerts for a space

Space-level Slack alerts notify a channel whenever specific events happen in that space. Only Space Admins and Workspace Owners can configure these.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Space">
    Navigate to the Space you want to configure alerts for and open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Space settings">
    Click the **gear icon** (⚙️) next to the space name, then click **Notification Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Slack channel">
    From the **Slack channel** dropdown, select the channel where alerts should be posted. Only channels that the Company Brain Slack app has been invited to will appear in this list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select alert events">
    Choose which events trigger a Slack alert:
    - **New items added** — a knowledge item is created in the space
    - **Items updated** — an existing item is edited
    - **New comments** — someone comments on an item in the space
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. Alerts will begin posting to your chosen Slack channel for future events.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## @mentions

Typing `@username` anywhere in a knowledge item's body or in a comment sends that user an **in-app notification** and an **email notification** (if they have email notifications enabled). Mentions are a lightweight way to:

- Assign action items directly within a knowledge item.
- Flag a piece of knowledge as relevant to a specific teammate.
- Loop in subject-matter experts during collaborative editing.

The mentioned user receives a link directly to the item and the exact location of the mention. You can mention multiple users in a single item by adding multiple `@username` references.

## Following items and spaces

Follow any item or space to receive notifications whenever it changes — without waiting to be @mentioned.

- **To follow an item** — open the item and click the **Follow** button near the top-right of the item view.
- **To follow a space** — open the space and click **Follow** next to the space name.

You'll receive in-app notifications (and email notifications, if enabled) whenever the followed item or space is updated, commented on, or has new items added.

To stop following, click the **Following** button (it turns blue when active) and select **Unfollow** from the dropdown.

<Tip>
For larger teams, weekly email digests strike the right balance: everyone stays informed about new knowledge without the interruption of real-time alerts. Enable the weekly digest for all members, and reserve Slack alerts and @mentions for genuinely time-sensitive updates.
</Tip>
