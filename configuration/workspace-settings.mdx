Workspace settings let you tailor Company Brain to your organization — from your company name and logo to security policies and default content language. Whether you're onboarding a new team or refining an existing setup, the Settings panel gives you full control over how your workspace looks, behaves, and protects your data.

## Accessing settings

Click your workspace name in the top-left corner of the app, then click **Settings** in the dropdown menu. Alternatively, navigate directly to `app.companybrain.ai/settings` in your browser.

## General settings

The **General** tab lets you configure the core identity and regional preferences of your workspace.

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Workspace name** | The display name for your organization, shown across the app and in email notifications. |
| **Workspace URL slug** | Your unique subdomain (e.g. `acme.companybrain.ai`). Changing this updates all shared links — notify your team before making changes. |
| **Logo (light version)** | Shown on light-mode backgrounds and in email footers. Recommended size: 200×48 px PNG or SVG. |
| **Logo (dark version)** | Shown when dark mode is active. Use a version of your logo that works on dark backgrounds. |
| **Default language** | Sets the default interface and spell-check language for all new members. Individual users can override this in their profile settings. |
| **Timezone** | Used for scheduled digests, audit log timestamps, and notification delivery times. |

After making changes, click **Save changes** at the bottom of the form.

## Security settings

The **Security** tab lets you define authentication and access policies for your entire workspace.

**Password policy**

- Set a minimum password length (default: 8 characters; recommended: 12+).
- Require complexity rules: uppercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

**Session timeout**

Configure how long an idle session stays active before users are automatically signed out. Options range from 15 minutes to 30 days.

**Allowed email domains**

Restrict new signups to specific email domains. For example, entering `yourcompany.com` means only users with a `@yourcompany.com` address can create an account in your workspace. Add multiple domains by separating them with commas.

**Two-factor authentication (2FA)**

- **Optional** — users can choose to enable 2FA in their profile settings.
- **Required** — all workspace members must enroll in 2FA before accessing any content. Users who haven't enrolled are prompted immediately on next login.

## Data & privacy

Company Brain is built with security and compliance at its core.

- **Encryption at rest** — all stored data is encrypted using AES-256.
- **Encryption in transit** — all data transmitted between your browser and Company Brain's servers uses TLS 1.3.
- **Compliance** — Company Brain is GDPR-compliant and holds SOC 2 Type II certification. You can request a copy of the audit report from your account manager.
- **Data residency** — choose whether your data is stored in the **US** or **EU** region. Data residency selection is available on the Enterprise plan and must be configured when the workspace is first created.

Your data is never used to train shared AI models. Each workspace's data is fully isolated.

## Billing

Navigate to **Settings → Billing** to manage your subscription and payment details.

From the **Plans** page you can:

- View your **current plan** and its included limits.
- Monitor **usage metrics** for the current billing period: total knowledge items, active members, and API calls made this month.
- **Upgrade or downgrade** your plan — changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle (downgrades) or immediately (upgrades).
- **Manage payment methods** — add, remove, or update your credit card or invoice details.

If you're on an annual plan, your renewal date and next invoice amount are shown at the top of the Billing page.

<Note>
Only **Workspace Owners** can access Billing settings or perform workspace-level destructive actions. Admins and Members do not have access to these sections.
</Note>

## Deleting a workspace

<Warning>
Deleting your workspace is **permanent and irreversible**. All knowledge items, spaces, integrations, member accounts, and associated data will be immediately and permanently removed. There is no recovery option. Export your data before proceeding (Settings → Data → Export).
</Warning>

To delete your workspace:

1. Go to **Settings → General**.
2. Scroll to the **Danger Zone** section at the bottom of the page.
3. Click **Delete Workspace**.
4. Type your workspace name to confirm.
5. Click **Permanently Delete**.

All data, integrations, and member access are permanently removed upon confirmation.
