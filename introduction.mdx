Company Brain is the knowledge management platform built for modern teams who are tired of hunting through Slack threads, Notion pages, GitHub issues, and shared drives to find the answer they need. By connecting all your existing tools in one place and layering AI-powered semantic search and an automatically generated knowledge graph on top, Company Brain turns your company's scattered collective intelligence into a single, instantly searchable hub. Whether you're a five-person startup or a five-thousand-person enterprise, Company Brain grows with you — keeping every decision, document, and discussion discoverable by everyone who needs it.

## How onboarding works

Getting your team up and running takes less than ten minutes. The four steps below walk you through everything — from creating your workspace to making your first AI-powered search — so you can start turning scattered knowledge into shared intelligence right away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your workspace">
    Sign up or sign in at [app.companybrain.ai](https://app.companybrain.ai) and click **New Workspace**. Give it a name (usually your company name), choose an icon, and invite your first teammates. Your workspace is your company's private, secure knowledge hub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your tools">
    Head to **Settings → Integrations** and connect the tools your team already uses — Slack, Notion, Google Drive, GitHub, Confluence, and more. Company Brain immediately begins indexing your existing content so nothing gets left behind.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your first knowledge items">
    Click **New Item** in the sidebar to write your first document in Markdown, upload a file, or paste a snippet. Add tags, assign it to a space, and hit **Publish** to make it searchable for your whole team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and discover">
    Press `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux) from anywhere in the app and ask a question in plain language. Company Brain surfaces the most relevant results — complete with AI-generated summaries and graph relationships — instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
